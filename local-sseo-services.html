What Is The Difference Between Local SEO And Traditional SEO?

The world of SEO can be divided into two distinct categories: local SEO and traditional SEO. Local search engine optimization is adapted towards localized search intent, such as "bakery near me," while traditional SEO focuses on non-localized searches, like "productivity app." It means that businesses that offer in-person services will typically employ local SEO strategies.

When it comes to tactics, local SEO and traditional SEO use different approaches. Local search engine optimization techniques often involve optimizing citation listings, such as Google Business Profile, to significantly impact local rankings. However, most companies invest in both traditional SEO and local SEO because they both have the power to drive revenue and enhance online visibility.
